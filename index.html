About Us

Co-Nation strives to help the local businesses that are hit hardest from this pandemic

Small business owners are one of the groups hardest hit by the pandemic. They are often very beloved and important to their local community; they define a neighbourhood’s character and have close relationships with regular customers. But they have a very difficult time staying in business when they cannot operate as usual. Our application gives the community a way to support their favourite businesses so that they won’t be forced to close.
